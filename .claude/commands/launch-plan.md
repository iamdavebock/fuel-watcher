# Launch Plan

Plan a time-boxed launch campaign (webinar, challenge, waitlist, or founding-member) and produce a day-by-day launch calendar with every asset assigned to an agent. Led by `launch`, executed by the Growth team.

---

## Inputs (gather first)

Ask all at once:

1. What's being launched, and is the offer finalised? (if not → `offer-architect` first)
2. Target date or window? Any hard constraints?
3. Audience size today: email list, social following, customer base?
4. Revenue target or sales count for the launch?
5. Budget: any paid promotion? (ad spend requires Dave's explicit approval)
6. Has this audience been launched to before? What happened?

---

## Build Sequence

1. **`launch`** — select the launch model (webinar / 5-day challenge / waitlist / founding-member) with justification, and draft the launch anatomy: runway → registration → event → cart open → cart close → post-launch
2. **`launch`** — produce the day-by-day calendar: every piece of content, email, and action with dates and owners
3. **Asset delegation:**
   - Registration page + cart-open/close emails → `copywriter`
   - Email flow wiring (confirmation, show-up sequence, cart sequence) → `email-lifecycle`
   - Runway content plan → `content-engine`
   - Paid promotion plan (if budget approved) → `media-buyer`
   - Sales call handling for high-ticket → `sales-closer`
   - Visuals → `marketing-creative`
4. **`growth`** — define the metrics dashboard: registrations, show rate, conversion, revenue vs target, and the post-launch debrief template

---

## Non-Negotiables

- [ ] Every deadline and scarcity claim is REAL and will be honoured
- [ ] Show-up rate engineering planned (confirmation sequence, reminders, open loops) — registration→attendance is the #1 lever
- [ ] Final-48-hours plan exists (typically ~50% of revenue)
- [ ] Post-launch path for non-buyers (downsell or nurture — never silence)
- [ ] All spend (ads, tools, SMS) approved by Dave before commitment
- [ ] Debrief scheduled before the launch starts

---

## Output

```
launch/
├── launch-plan.md      # model, anatomy, targets, risks
├── calendar.md         # day-by-day: date, asset, channel, owner, status
├── asset-briefs/       # one brief per delegated asset
└── debrief-template.md # filled in after cart close
```

Finish by stating the single biggest risk to this launch and its mitigation.
